About me...

I am a high-school student in Canada, and I wish to share my joy with ACG, amateur radio and retro computing with you.

My favorite games are mostly slow-paced and open world, including Minecraft, Genshin Impact, Euro Truck Simulator 2 and Transport Fever 2.

I have been consistently learning to mess up with everything about computer ever since my early years. There is just too much to experience in this wonderful technology world.
I am also a new radio ham licensed from April 2024, with my call signs being VA3LPZ and BI4LPZ. If you are also a ham, feel free to get in touch with me, VY 73!

If you are not one, why not consider joining this intriguing hobby that more than 3 million people in the world enjoys! Amateur radio helps you gain skills in many different tech fields, and joining the great society is also a great way to make new friends all over the world.
